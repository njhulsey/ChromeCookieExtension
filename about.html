Cookie Control Center



Manage:

Cookie Control Center aims to bring control to users by allowing them to view and edit cookies found on their device. On the home page, users can search for and lookup cookies. With the lookup feature, all of the data found in the cookie will be displayed on the table. Users can remember cookies to keep them from being deleted. Remembered cookies will appear in the about page. Cookies may be deleted when individually looked up. To delete multiple types of cookies at once use the search feature and click the delete all found button at the bottom of the page.

Learn More:

Cookie Control Center attempts to associate cookies with companies associated with them. Users can look up what each cookie does by clicking on the name column in the data table. By providing this transparency to users, Cookie Control Center hopes to force accountability on companies and educate the user about cookie functionality.
